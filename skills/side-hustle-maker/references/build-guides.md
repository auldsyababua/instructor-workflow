## Key Themes Across All Guides

### 1. Ship Fast, Learn Faster
All guides emphasize rapid iteration over perfection. Launch in weeks, not months.

### 2. Validate Early
Don't build for months without customer feedback. Talk to users in Week 1.

### 3. Focus on Distribution
The best product means nothing without users. Distribution = success.

### 4. Mental Health Matters
Solo founding is lonely. Build systems, routines, and community early.

### 5. Sustainable > Explosive
Better to build something that lasts 10 years than burn out in year 1.

### 6. Learn from Failures
All successful founders have multiple failed projects. It's part of the process.
